How to Play
The Trench · Solo Dive

🐋 The Dive

Guide your whale down to the treasure chest on the seabed. Your oxygen drains constantly and every move costs a breath — so pick your path, don't thrash around. Crack the chest open and a water portal tears open right beside it: step into the portal to descend to the next level. Cracking the chest tops up your air too. 100 levels down.

🦈 Predators by Habitat

Different depths, different hunters — all of them patrol the lanes inside swirling currents. Touch one and it knocks you back toward the surface and costs air. Time your moves through the gaps.

🦈 Sharkswarm shallows — they chase you when you're close
🐻‍❄️ Polar BearsArctic ice — they ambush whales here
🫎 Mooseisland channels — orcas hunt them in open water
🦑 Giant Squidthe deep — pale ghosts, a sperm whale's nemesis
👁️ Colossal Squidthe Abyss — bigger, beaked, shrugs off your sonar

🫧 Air Bubbles

Pockets of trapped air drift in the calm water. Swim into one to grab a breath and restore oxygen — a lifeline on the deeper, busier levels.

🪼 Jellyfish

Translucent jellies drift through the water. Brush one and it gives a light sting — a small nip of oxygen, no real harm. Annoying in a crowd, not deadly.

🪨 Rocks

Boulders block their cell — you can't swim through them, so plan your path around them.

🐬 Dolphin Allies

You're not alone down here. Pods of dolphins streak across the trench and stun every predator in a lane as they pass — watch for one and dart through the lane it clears.

🌊 Echolocation (the final 20 levels)

In the Abyss the water goes black. Like a real sperm whale, you ping sonar — an expanding ring that lights the field and freezes the single nearest predator lane. Moose swim at the surface, so sonar can't touch them. Time your moves to each ping.

🦑 Kraken Boss (every 20 levels)

At levels 20, 40, 60, 80 and 100 a giant Kraken blocks the trench. It chases you and hurls lightning down columns — dodge to a dark column before it strikes. Whirlpools flicker in and out across the arena: lead the Kraken onto one for big damage (but they'll suck you in too, so don't linger). And let the dolphin allies ram it — each hit briefly freezes the Kraken. Drain its health bar to break through. Deeper Krakens get nastier: more lightning columns at once, rock obstacles, and roaming sharks.
